Accessibility Policy

Our commitment to making our website accessible to everyone.

Our Commitment

Elite Realty Group is committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to people with disabilities. We continually work to improve the accessibility and usability of our site, and strive to adhere to Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards.

Accessibility Features

Our website includes the following accessibility features: semantic HTML structure, keyboard-navigable content, descriptive alt text for images, sufficient color contrast ratios, resizable text, and accessible form labels and controls.
