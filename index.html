What We Do

Full-Service Real Estate

From first-time buyers to seasoned investors, we provide expert guidance at every stage of your real estate journey.

Buyer Representation

Dedicated buyer agents who know the Jacksonville market inside and out. We handle search, negotiation, inspections, and closing, so you can focus on finding the perfect home.

Listing & Selling

Strategic pricing, professional marketing, and skilled negotiation to get your property sold quickly and for top dollar. Our proven process maximizes your return on every transaction.

Investment Services

Whether you're acquiring your first rental or building a portfolio, we identify high-yield opportunities and connect you with our property management team at SUPRA PM.

Market Analysis

Data-driven comparative market analysis for buyers and sellers. Understand property values, neighborhood trends, and market timing to make confident decisions.

Transaction Management

Expert coordination of contracts, disclosures, inspections, appraisals, and closing. We keep every detail on track so nothing falls through the cracks.

Property Management

Through our sister company SUPRA Property Management, we offer full-service property management for investors, from tenant screening to maintenance to financial reporting.
