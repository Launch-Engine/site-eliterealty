Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about buying, selling, and investing in Jacksonville real estate.

Buying

Buyer FAQ

The best first step is a free consultation with one of our agents. We'll discuss your goals, budget, timeline, and preferred neighborhoods. We'll also connect you with trusted mortgage lenders to get pre-approved, which strengthens your buying position.

While you can browse listings at any time, getting pre-approved before touring homes is highly recommended. It tells sellers you're a serious buyer and helps you understand exactly what you can afford. We can recommend trusted local lenders.

In most cases, the seller pays the buyer's agent commission, so there is no direct cost to you for our representation. We'll explain all the details during your consultation so there are no surprises.

We serve the entire Jacksonville metro area, including Riverside, Avondale, San Marco, the Beaches, Mandarin, Southside, Ponte Vedra, St. Augustine, and Palm Coast. We also serve the Palm Beach and Lake Worth areas in South Florida.

For investment properties, we recommend buying from the smaller to medium size in the tract and spending only a minimal amount on upgrades. For your personal home, select upgrades that enhance your quality of living. One consistent recommendation: always upgrade the carpet and padding.

Selling

Seller FAQ

We provide a free Comparative Market Analysis (CMA) that evaluates your home against recent sales of similar properties in your area. This data-driven approach ensures your home is priced competitively to attract buyers while maximizing your return.

Market conditions vary, but a well-priced and properly marketed home in the Jacksonville area typically receives offers within the first few weeks. Our marketing strategy combines professional photography, online listings, social media, and our local network to maximize exposure.

We provide a detailed preparation checklist during our listing consultation. Key steps include decluttering, deep cleaning, minor repairs, and staging. Our team can recommend trusted local contractors and stagers to help your home show its best.
