About Elite Realty Group

Pursuing dreams together, one family, one investor, one transaction at a time.

Our Story

Built on Relationships

Elite Realty Group brings over 30 years of combined real estate experience to the Jacksonville market. We use advanced technology, experienced agents, and the latest in marketing techniques to help buyers find their next home, sellers get the best value, and investors grow their portfolios.

As members of NAR, NEFAR, and NARPM, we maintain the highest professional standards. Our sister company, SUPRA Property Management, provides full-service property management, giving our investor clients a seamless experience from purchase to management.
